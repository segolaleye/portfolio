Title: Leveraging NLP for Enhanced Insurance Processes in Africa: A Comprehensive Study on Sales, Claims, and Underwriting Automation

Journal: TBA

Year: TBA

Summary:

This research paper explores the application of Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to improve insurance processes in Africa, focusing on sales, claims, and underwriting. The study addresses unique challenges faced by the African insurance industry, such as linguistic diversity, limited technological infrastructure, and the prevalence of unstructured data.

The paper presents case studies from various African countries, demonstrating the practical implementation and impact of these NLP techniques. It also discusses the ethical considerations and regulatory compliance aspects of using AI in insurance processes within the African market. The research concludes with recommendations for insurance companies operating in Africa and suggests future directions for NLP applications in the industry, emphasizing the potential for increased insurance penetration and improved customer experiences across the continent.
